FAQs:


How do I ask a question?


  • Click I HAVE A QUESTION on the home page.
  • Type in your question.
  • Add any details that will help people answer it.
  • Select the most relevant category for your question.
  • If the question looks okay to you, click Submit.

How do I answer a question?


  • Go to any question in the Recent/Popular section or I CAN ANSWER section.
  • Enter your answer in the Answer field and click Submit.

How does level system work?


  • Your current level is displayed on the home page and you can always check your score by clicking the level at home page.
  • By clicking level systems in the menu, you can view the score you need to proceed to the next level.
